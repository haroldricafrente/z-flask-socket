About Milky Mushroom

Milky Mushrooms (Calocybe indica) are a robust and highly nutritious variety known for their thick, meaty texture and mild flavor. They are widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions due to their high-temperature tolerance. In agriculture, they provide a sustainable source of protein and essential nutrients, making them a valuable addition to diets worldwide.


Optimal Growing Conditions

Temperature: 25-35°C (77-95°F)

Humidity: 80-90%

CO₂ Levels: Below 1000 ppm

Light Intensity: Moderate indirect light
