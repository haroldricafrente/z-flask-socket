About White Oyster Mushroom

White Oyster Mushrooms (Pleurotus ostreatus) are widely cultivated for their delicate texture and mild, savory flavor. These mushrooms are known for their rapid growth and adaptability, making them a popular choice for both commercial and home cultivation. They also play a crucial role in biodegradation, breaking down organic matter and enriching soil health.


Optimal Growing Conditions

Temperature: 18-24°C (64-75°F)

Humidity: 85-95%

CO₂ Levels: Below 1000 ppm

Light Intensity: Moderate indirect light
